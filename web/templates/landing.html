stage

Observability for multi-agent reinforcement learning.

Stage records what happens inside an ensemble run. Every message, tool call, state change, and cost accumulation streams to a persistent trace as the scenario executes. When the run finishes, the trace is searchable, shareable, and queryable through a web UI. A run URL appears in your terminal the moment the scenario starts, before the first event has even been sent. You can open it and watch the evaluation unfold in real time, or come back later and step through every decision the agent made.

Sweeps group runs that vary a single parameter across multiple cells -- different models, different seeds, different worlds -- so you can compare outcomes in one place without assembling spreadsheets. Training runs record each fine-tuning job alongside its hyperparameters and per-step metrics, connected to the evaluation sweeps that preceded and followed them. The result is a structured record of what your agents did, across every run, every sweep, and every training iteration.
